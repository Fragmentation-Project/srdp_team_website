Tactics, non-violent

Overview

This dataset describes each self-determination organization’s use of non-violent tactics in each year. Non-violent tactics identified are: economic non-cooperation; protests or demonstrations; non-violent intervention; social non-cooperation; and political non-cooperation. We also identify instances of institutional participation.
